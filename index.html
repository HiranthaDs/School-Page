About Us

At St. Sebastian College, we believe in providing high-quality education, fostering holistic development, and nurturing the potential of each student. Our dedicated team of teachers ensures a balanced approach, preparing students for a bright future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in their academic journey and beyond. We focus on the all-round development of our students through academic excellence, extracurricular activities, and character-building programs.

Our Achievements

  • Consistent excellent performance in national exams
  • State-of-the-art facilities for students
